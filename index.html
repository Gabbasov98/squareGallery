About

Alexandra Veremeenko

I graduated from Pirogov Art School in 2019. I am a student of the famous Russian painter - Sergei Vlasov.

I moved to the United States in 2020. It so happened that I do not hear all emotions and experiences from birth. I express my vision of the world with the help of painting. I exhibited my works at various exhibitions in Russia.

Most of my work is done in oils. I like to depict landscapes, sometimes urbanism, still life, and many other areas to find myself and my technique.

I would like to present some of my work to show the different techniques and styles that I used to create canvases.
